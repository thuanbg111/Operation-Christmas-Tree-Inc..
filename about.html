About Operation Christmas Tree

OPERATION CHRISTMAS TREE, INC. is incorporated in Delaware and operates as an exempt charitable corporation (EIN 85-4273692).

What we do: bring holiday relief to families in need by coordinating tree & gift sponsorships, food boxes, and seasonal essentials with trusted local partners.

  • Committed to transparency, dignity, and efficient use of resources.
  • Partner‑first model: we equip local schools, churches, and nonprofits to serve.
  • All donations are used for charitable purposes consistent with our mission.
